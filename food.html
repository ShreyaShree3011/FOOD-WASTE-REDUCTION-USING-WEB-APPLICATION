Welcome   to   Hungry   Harvest

Food is essential for human existence. Deeply disturbing factor is that lakhs of people are suffering from hunger everyday. The main reason is that the lack of food they receive. On the other side lots of foods are being wasted. In recent years there has been increase in food waste due to factors such as in over production and expired dates. The main aim of this website to facilitate food redistribution as an innovative solution to tackle food waste and food poverty. Hungry Harvest enables the effective distribution of excess foods and essential items. Distribution of excess foods helps in reduction of food waste. Focusing on developing easy to use website, we have created Hungry Harvest.

The main aim of Hungry Harvest is donation of excess food to the needy people and help them. Donating food helps in reducing food waste. Food is a basic need. Hungry Harvest focuses on donation of dry foods, such as sprouts, grains, snacks and dry fruits. Dry foods have longer life compared to fresh food. Many dry foods are nutritious, provides good source of protein.

It also encourages, charitable givings. By making it smooth and convenient to donate food. Users can also donate various others items such as clothes, books and blankets.
