Hungry   Harvest   Overview

Food is essential for every human. Food helps to maintain healthy and balanced life. Many people simply cannot afford to buy enough food to meet their basic needs, due to very low-income. In recent years, there has been raise in food waste. Hungry Harvest offers a helping hand to the needy. Hungry Harvest allows donors to donate various items,such as books, clothes, and blankets, along with the details of their donation. This website makes it easy for people to donate food. They can do so from the comfort of their own place and time. This can encourage more people to donate. Our website can help increase food security by ensuring people have access to nutritious food. Hungry harvest mainly focuses on donation of nutritious foods such as dry fruits, food grains and sprouts. Life of these foods are longer compared to fresh foods, which makes the donation easy. Overall, this website helps in reducing food waste and help the people who have less access to food.
